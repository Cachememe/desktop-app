How it works ‚öô

The idea is pretty fly, we Cache images once and append text dynamically to each image.



Since we download plain text which requires just a few kilobytes, Cacheme.me will be there for you even when your mobile connection has been throttled and you are under a rock in the middle of nowhere.

Advantages:
1. Requires almost no data volume!
2. Requires almost no storage space!
3. Reliable despite bad connection!

‚ö° Data Usage

{# After the Memes (around 500kb) got Cached, only very small data chunks of text#} {#(1kb-10kb) get transferred.
#} Even in 2018 a huge amount of people around the globe do not have access to a 3G internet connection and are trapped inside a memeingless world.

Beside the revolutionary low data meme format, our goal was to minimize the data usage on the entire platform, to make entertainment accessible to everybody. We achieved this by renouncing high quality images, user behavior analysis and fancy web design (we simply kept it core AF). To put this in numbers: Your average website has more than 2.300kb, while we stick with 13kb.




Example from the Light Version with 100 loaded posts, only 15kb were transferred in total.
Each "Type XHR" are 15 posts, the images are already "Cached" and do not transfer any data at all.
